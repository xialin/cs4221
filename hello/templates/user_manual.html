User Manual


What are the precedures to follow?

  • First, click "Upload File" on the navigation bar.
  • Second, click "Browse" to upload your ER-Diagram from your local file. Make sure it is in XML format. Then click "Submit"
  • Then, you can see your uploaded file in the textarea. Click "convert" to start conversion
  • If there is need to select a primary key, you will be prompted with the options.
  • You may also be prompted to decide on merging tables.
  • After these steps, your ER-Diagram will be converted to JSON format.
